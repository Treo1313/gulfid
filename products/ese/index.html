Lightning Protection

We offer Early Streamer Emmission Lightning Protection

We offer a range of products from a number of manufacturers: Duval-Messien, Protart and Dameks. All products conform to NFC17-102.

StormSat® ESE from Duval Messien, France

Features:

  • Intelligent static generator able to ground both +ve and –ve strikes equally and simultaneously

  • E-counter and e-analysis of lightning strikes

  • Advance information regarding storm activity

  • Information regarding lightning activity and Lightning current

  • Information regarding lightning risk evolution

  • Long-term history with date and time of lightning strike.

  • Online and real time information to preferred output device (e.g. Tablet, mobile) via wi-fi.

  • Test of StormSAT®: battery, solar panels and electronic circuit

Satelit3 ESE from Duval Messien, France

Features:

  • Intelligent static generator able to ground both +ve and –ve strikes equally and simultaneously

  • 4 levels of testing through hand held remote tester at all weather conditions

  • Sealed in 304L stainless steel shell
