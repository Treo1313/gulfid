TEREC+ Permanent Earth Enhancing Compound

Terec+ is the latest solution for safe and permanent earthing and complies with IEEE 80:2000, clause 14.5.d. The compound sharply reduces soil resistivity and does not require periodic maintenance ensuring safety and peace of mind.

  • Miracle compound consisting of rare natural composites

  • Highly Electrically & Thermally conductive

  • Achieves resistance acceptable to any international body or local authorities (e.g. MEDC, OETC)

  • Saves delicate electrical and electronic and mechanical equipment from ground faults

  • Maintains low Step, Touch and Mesh Potential

  • Inert in nature and environmentally friendly

  • Sharp reduction in installation, space and maintenance requirement
